Our Lady of Victory Parish Youth Group
Join Youth Group
Join Our Lady of Victory's teens for monthly opportunities to connect and serve the community. Our mission is to keep highschool students engaged with the parish and each other by working to earn service hours and have fun.
Parental Permission
Your child's safety and well-being are important, so all Youth Group activities are supervised by VIRTUS-compliant parents. By enrolling your teen you agree to allow them to participate in events and activities both on and off-site. You also agree to hold Our Lady of Victory, its employees, and volunteers harmless for any claims the may arise in connection with your teen's participation in Youth Group activities.
